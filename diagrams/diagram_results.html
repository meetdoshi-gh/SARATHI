Speed experiment insight: With engine-force range expanded from [0.1, 0.125] to [0.1, 0.25], SAC failed to learn because it filled the replay buffer with low-reward, low-speed samples and couldn't escape the plateau. RSAC, with sequential context T=16, learned to leverage higher speeds to cover more track — validating the temporal-awareness hypothesis even though full RSAC tuning was time-constrained. This experiment was deliberately designed to isolate the contribution of the LSTM.
